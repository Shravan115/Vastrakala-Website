Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order status by logging into your account or contacting our customer care team.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all our products. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it in its original condition for a full refund or exchange.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location.

How do I care for my traditional garments?

Each product comes with detailed care instructions. Generally, we recommend dry cleaning for silk and embroidered pieces, and gentle hand washing for cotton items.

Can I customize my order?

Yes, we offer customization services for many of our products. Please contact us at least 2 weeks before your desired delivery date for custom orders.

How do you support artisans?

We pay our artisans 3-4 times the market rate, provide healthcare benefits, and invest in community development. 10% of our profits go back to artisan communities.
